Oxy Tigers First Win!

Russell Whealdon's 2 points helped secure a 55 point win over Bethesda University on Sunday

The Occidental Mens Basketball team opened the 2018-19 season Sunday afternoon, cruising to a 106-55 win in an exhibition matchup against Bethesda University. The Tigers showed no signs of rust, scoring 60 points in the first half — shooting an efficient 51 percent from the field. Jonny Crosthwaite had a huge half with 21 points, going 3-4 from beyond the arch, finishing with 23 points and eight assists. Jacob Adler caught fire in the second half, finishing with 23 points on 5 for 10 shooting. Zach Baines ended with 21 points and 3 blocks, and Austin DeWitz added an easy 18 points, 13 rebounds, and seven assists. Caleb Yellin-Flaherty looked good on defense, posting 12 boards, two steals, and two blocks. Ryan Kaneshiro played great on-ball defense and got people great open looks with seven assists and just one turnover. Oxy as a group looked its best when it made the extra pass, assisting on 33 of its baskets.
